About

The man... The myth... The legend...

Hi my name is Andrew and I am a software engineer.

When the pandemic hit I decided to make the most of my time and I tried a Codecademy course. I soon devoured their Python 2 course and continued on to Python 3. By the time I finished ‘Automate the Boring Stuff’ I knew I had to take this to the next level.
I applied and was accepted to App Academy, a thousand hour full stack bootcamp, where I honed my skills and became a software engineer. My time as an artistic creative has developed my mental flexibility as well as my ability to work in any group. I look forward to using those skills to help you achieve even greater heights!
